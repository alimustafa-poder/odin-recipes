Recipe Nihari


Description

Nihari Masala recipe by Gulzar Hussain. This delightful Latest recipe of Nihari Masala can be ready in Minutes and good to serve around People. Follow all the steps recommended by Chef to get a perfect dish.

Ingredients

  • Badaan phool.......4
  • Bari ellachi......4
  • Saaunth .......1 tsp.
  • Haldi........ 1 tsp.
  • Salt.....1tsp.
  • Tatri............ 1/2tsp.
  • Zeera........2tsp.
  • Jaifel.....1/2tsp.
  • Javetri.....1/2tsp.
  • Pisa dhania......2tsp.
  • Pisi lal mirch......2tsp.
  • Saouf........2tsp.

Method

Sabut masalay ko boohn ker pees lain or dosy masalay mai mix ker ky istimaal karain.
