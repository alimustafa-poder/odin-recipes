Recipe Pulao


Description

Treat your taste buds with delicious PULAO recipe. Prepare it with ease at home for family or friends. The women searching for unique and delicious recipes online can try PULAO for the weekend.

Ingredients

  • ONION 2
  • OIL TO TASTE
  • RED CHILI TO TASTE
  • SALT TO TASTE
  • RICE 1 KG
  • MEAT 1KG

Method

  1. OIL IN PAN
  2. THEN PUT ONIONS.
  3. STIR TILL IT BECOMES BROWN.
  4. THEN PUT MEAT READ CHILII SALT .
  5. STIR THEM FOR 15 MINUTES.
  6. THEN PUT WATER AS YOU REQUIRED THEN ADD RICE
